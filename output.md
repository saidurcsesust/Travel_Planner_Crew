## Day-wise Itinerary
### Day 1: 2026-04-10
- Morning: Explore Istanbul's Sultanahmet district on a self-guided walking tour
- Afternoon: Visit the Topkapi Palace and enjoy traditional Turkish tea
- Evening: Join the Istanbul Food and Culture Tour
- Estimated spend: ~83.35 USD

### Day 2: 2026-04-11
- Morning: Take a low-cost ferry ride along the Bosphorus Strait and visit the Spice Bazaar
- Afternoon: Visit the Basilica Cistern and explore the nearby streets for local street food
- Evening: Enjoy a traditional Turkish bath experience
- Estimated spend: ~55.90 USD

### Day 3: 2026-04-12
- Morning: Fly to Cappadocia and check-in to a budget-friendly hotel
- Afternoon: Explore the Goreme Open-Air Museum and take a short hike
- Evening: Enjoy a low-cost dinner and watch the sunset
- Estimated spend: ~267.90 USD

### Day 4: 2026-04-13
- Morning: Take a hot air balloon ride over Cappadocia
- Afternoon: Visit the Derinkuyu Underground City and explore the nearby village
- Evening: Enjoy a traditional Turkish dinner and relax at the hotel
- Estimated spend: ~107.35 USD

### Day 5: 2026-04-14
- Morning: Fly back to Istanbul and spend the morning shopping for souvenirs
- Afternoon: Visit the Istanbul Archaeological Museum and enjoy a low-cost lunch
- Evening: Depart from Istanbul
- Estimated spend: ~70.05 USD
